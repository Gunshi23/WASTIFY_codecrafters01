Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trash to Treasure?

+
Trash to Treasure is an innovative online marketplace dedicated to connecting people who create unique products from recycled materials with conscious consumers looking for sustainable alternatives.

How do I join?

+
Click "Join the Movement", fill out a quick form, and you can start browsing or listing upcycled creations. Basic membership is free!

Can I sell my own upcycled products?

+
Absolutely! Create a seller profile, upload product details, set prices, and connect with buyers.

How does shipping work?

+
Sellers set shipping options. We encourage eco-friendly and local pickup methods to reduce the carbon footprint.

Do you offer upcycling workshops?

+
Yes! We regularly host online and in-person workshops. Check the Events section for updates.
